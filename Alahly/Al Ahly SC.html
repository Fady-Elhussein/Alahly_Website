Al Ahly SC 🦅

Al Ahly Sporting Club (Arabic: النادي الأهلي الرياضي‎, literal translation: The National Club), commonly referred to as Al Ahly, is an Egyptian professional sports club based in Cairo, and is considered as the most successful team in Africa and as one of the continent's giants. It is best known for its professional football team that plays in the Egyptian Premier League, the top tier in the Egyptian football league system. It is one of the most decorated football clubs in the world. It was founded on 24 April 1907, as a gathering place for Cairo's Student Unions.
